## Full description

### English (Google Play / App Store)

```
ChildrenEng helps children learn English in a playful way.

LEARN WORDS
• 19 picture categories: animals, colors, fruits, family, weather, seasons, and more
• Alphabet and numbers
• Tap any tile to hear the word spoken clearly in English
• Colorful, kid-friendly design with gentle animations

GAMES
• Write letters — trace letters with your finger
• Math game — simple addition and subtraction
• Count animals — how many do you see?
• Memory — match pairs of pictures
• Listen and tap — hear a word, pick the right picture
• Where is — drag items to the right place
• Sort it — sort pictures into the correct baskets

DESIGNED FOR CHILDREN
• Large buttons and bright visuals
• Friendly voice with text-to-speech
• Haptic feedback on supported devices
• No ads, no account required, no data collection
• Works offline

Perfect for preschoolers and early learners starting their English journey.
```
